F. A. Q.


When will my order ship?

Orders are shipped within 3-5 business days and will arrive within 7 days if delivery location is in the United States. International orders will arrive within 10-14 days.


How much are shipping fees?

For domestic orders in the U.S., there is a flat shipping rate of $8.00. Shipments to Europe and South America will cost $10.00. All other countries will cost $15.00 to ship.


How do I cancel my order?

Orders may only be cancelled before recieving a shipment notification. To cancel an order please email us with the subject line: "CANCEL ORDER". Provide your order number that was emailed to you in your order confirmation and your reason for cancelling. You will then receive a cancelled order confirmation email from our team.


My order says delivered, but I have not recieved it.

After shipment, we are not responisble for your garment. Please contact the courrier from the shipment confirmation for issues regarding the shipped package.


Do you accept returns?

Yes, returns are accepted with cause. If you are dissatisfied with your purchase, please email our team with the subject line: "RETURN". In the email please attatch a photo of the garment and describe the reason for returning.
