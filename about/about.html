About Us

"Delicates" was founded by California-based fashion merchandiser Kim Nguyen in dedication to her passion for the longevity of clothing. She believes that proper care of clothing is one of the most important aspects of fashion sustainability. It is also a mode of encouraging others to refrain from poor-quality, fast-fashion purchases by showing the worth of paying more for high-quality garments.

by Kimberly Nguyen 2024
